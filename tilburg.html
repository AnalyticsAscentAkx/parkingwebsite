The complete guide to parking in Tilburg

Tilburg is North Brabant's largest city and the sixth biggest city in the Netherlands. Known for its industrial heritage, design culture, and Tilburg University, it's a city that's been quietly reinventing itself. The Piushaven neighbourhood — once a working harbour — is now one of the trendiest dining and nightlife areas in the region.

Piushaven — best value and best location

Parking Piushaven at €1.50/hr is not just the cheapest central garage, it's also the most useful — putting you right next to the Piushaven restaurants, terraces, and just a 10-minute walk from the main shopping streets.

013 and the music scene

Tilburg has a famous music scene centred around the 013 venue. For evening events, most nearby street parking is free after 21:00. Parking 013 and Parking Spoorlaan are both close, and evenings are free for street parking.

Efteling day trips

Tilburg is the closest city to the Efteling theme park (about 25km away). If you're combining a Tilburg visit with Efteling, the P+R at Koningshoeven is a sensible base as it's on the south side of the city closest to the park.
