The complete guide to parking in Groningen

Groningen is one of the Netherlands' most liveable cities with a large student population. The Grote Markt and Vismarkt are the heart of the city. Street parking is €3.40/hr but the P+R system at just €2/day makes it very affordable.

Best city for P+R value

Groningen's P+R costs just €2/24hr — the cheapest in the Netherlands after Amsterdam. With 4 facilities around the ring road, you can always find a spot. Kardinge (east) is largest and rarely full.

Cycling culture

Groningen is the Netherlands' cycling capital. Many visitors park at P+R and rent a bike (€8-12/day) to explore the city. This is often faster than taking a bus.

Forum Groningen

The Forum cultural centre has its own underground parking. If you're visiting Forum, this is most convenient, though pricier at €2.50/hr.
