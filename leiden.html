The complete guide to parking in Leiden

Leiden is a compact historic university city with strict parking enforcement. The binnenstad (historic centre) around the canals and Rapenburg is €3.50/hr — 24/7 on weekdays but free on Sundays.

University zone warnings

During university exam periods (January, June), parking demand spikes. The Lammermarkt P+R fills by 9am. Arrive early or use Haagweg instead.

Sunday is the secret

All of Leiden's paid zones are free on Sundays. If you're visiting for tourism, Sunday parking is completely free throughout the city. Museums like Naturalis and the Rijksmuseum van Oudheden are open Sunday, making it the perfect day trip.

Near the station

Leiden Centraal station area (Stationsomgeving) is €3.00/hr. Use the station parking garage or take a train from a nearby P+R.
