How to use this map

This map shows curated parking spots across the Netherlands. Each marker has type-coded colour, live rate, and tips. Click any marker for the full breakdown and a direct link to the relevant city guide.

  • Green = Free parking — areas with no charge any day, evenings, or Sundays.
  • Teal = P+R — Park & Ride from €1/day with return public transport.
  • Orange = Garage — paid garages with the best day-rate value.
  • Purple = Blue Zone — free for 1-2 hours with a parking disc (€2 at petrol stations).

Why these spots

Most parking apps surface only the nearest garage at full rate. We rank by genuine savings: a P+R location 8 minutes from the centre saves €40+ per day vs the closest centre garage. The map highlights those wins.

What we don't show

We deliberately exclude generic street meters (every city has them — use the street parking guide instead) and full-price city-centre garages that nobody serious should pick. This map is the curated shortlist.
