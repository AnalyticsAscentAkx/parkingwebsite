Parking in Eindhoven

Eindhoven is a breath of fresh air for parking — €3.30/hr in the centre with free evenings and Sundays. The tech hub's parking is genuinely budget-friendly compared to the Randstad.

Evening visits are free

Unlike Amsterdam's 24/7 paid parking, Eindhoven stops charging at 18:00 in most zones. Perfect for dinner, Strijp-S nightlife, or evening visits to the Van Abbemuseum.
