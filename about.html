About Parking Netherlands

Independent parking intelligence. No paywalls. No hidden agendas. Just the most accurate, useful parking data for the Netherlands.

What is Parking Netherlands?

Parking Netherlands is an independent, data-driven platform that helps visitors, expats, and locals find the best parking options across the Netherlands. We compare garages, P+R locations, street parking zones, and free parking across all major Dutch cities.

This is not a static price list. We track rate changes, update data monthly, and provide context that generic search results don't — like transit times, zone rules, local tricks, and the exact strategies that save money.

Who built this?

Parking Netherlands is built and maintained by Analytics Ascent — a data analytics and digital intelligence company. We use data-driven methods to build useful, accurate tools for real-world decisions.

The founder has lived across the Netherlands and has navigated Amsterdam's €8/hr parking, the Schiphol airport parking nightmare, and the Maastricht Sunday miracle firsthand. This site exists because existing parking resources were either out of date, behind paywalls, or missed the strategic picture.

Data sources

We use the following legal, open data sources:

  • OpenStreetMap via Overpass API — parking locations, charging stations
  • Municipality open data portals — Amsterdam Data, Rotterdam Open Data, Den Haag Open Data
  • Official municipal tariff publications — parking zone rates updated annually
  • NDVW/NDW — Dutch National Data Warehouse for traffic and mobility data
  • OpenChargeMap — EV charging station data

We do not scrape commercial operators (ParkBee, Q-Park, APCOA). We do not bypass APIs. We respect rate limits and attribution requirements.

How often is data updated?

Parking rates are reviewed quarterly or when municipalities announce changes. Map data is refreshed monthly from OSM. If you notice an error, please contact us.

Is this site really free?

Yes. 100% free. No account required. No paywalls. We earn through affiliate links (when you click "Check Availability" and book) and display advertising. Sponsored placement is clearly marked as "Featured".
