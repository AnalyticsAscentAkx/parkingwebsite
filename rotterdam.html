Parking guide for Rotterdam

Rotterdam is significantly cheaper than Amsterdam for parking, with centre street rates at €4.24/hr. Better yet, many zones offer free weekend parking — something Amsterdam doesn't have. If you do get caught without payment, Dutch parking fines start at €82.00 plus the unpaid fee.

P+R saves real money

Rotterdam's P+R locations start at €2.50/24hr with fast metro connections. Kralingse Zoom is the top pick — 10 minutes by metro to the centre.

Free on Sundays

Most Rotterdam zones don't charge on Sundays. If you're visiting for a day trip, Sunday is the day to come. Even on weekdays, rates are roughly half of Amsterdam's.
