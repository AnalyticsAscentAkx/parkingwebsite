Parking in Utrecht

Utrecht surprises many visitors with its €5.10/hr centre rate — second only to Amsterdam. The compact, canal-lined centre makes driving in unnecessary anyway.

P+R is more expensive here

Unlike Amsterdam's €1 P+R, Utrecht charges €5/day. Still far cheaper than street parking, and P+R Westraven has 800 spots — the largest in the city.
