The complete guide to parking in Breda

Breda is a mid-sized Brabant city that punches above its weight for culture and dining. With the magnificent Grote Kerk (Great Church) and the Nassau-Baronie castle, it's a popular day trip destination. Parking is affordable compared to Randstad cities.

Chasse is your best central bet

Parking Chasse at €1.50/hr is the best value central garage. It's a short walk from the Grote Markt, the Kasteel, and all main attractions. The 'Chasse' area is also a cultural hub with a theatre.

Sunday trips

Like most Dutch cities, all Breda paid zones are free on Sundays. The Grote Markt hosts a weekly market on Wednesday mornings — if visiting then, arrive early before street spots fill up.

NHTV/Avans student city

During university periods (September-June), the centre is busier. The P+R at Claudius Prinsenlaan is reliable even on busy days.
