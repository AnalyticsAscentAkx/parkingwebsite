The complete guide to parking in Zwolle

Zwolle is one of the best-kept secrets in the Netherlands for day trippers. The medieval Binnenstad (inner city) has been beautifully preserved, with the 14th-century Sassenpoort gate, a network of canals, and a vibrant dining scene — yet it draws far fewer crowds than Amsterdam or Utrecht. Parking is among the most affordable of any Dutch city.

Lübeckplein — the best value garage

At just €1.40/hr, Parking Lübeckplein is the cheapest central garage in the city and puts you right next to the main shopping streets and the historic centre. For a full day, it's hard to beat at €10.

The Sassenpoort and Binnenstad

Zwolle's Sassenpoort (14th century gate tower) is the most photographed sight in the city. The garages at Rodetorenplein and Lübeckplein are both within a 5-minute walk. On Sundays these areas are free — and the Binnenstad market on Saturdays fills the streets.

Zwolle as a base for exploring Overijssel

Zwolle is a major rail junction. Trains run to Groningen, Amsterdam, Arnhem, Almelo and Leeuwarden. Parking at P+R Hanzeland and taking the train outward is an efficient strategy for exploring eastern Netherlands.
