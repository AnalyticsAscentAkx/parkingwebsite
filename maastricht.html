The complete guide to parking in Maastricht

Maastricht is unique in the Netherlands — a southern city with a distinct Burgundian character, Romanesque architecture, and strong Belgian/German influence. The Vrijthof and Grote Staat are the heart of the city. Parking costs €3.60/hr in the centre.

Sunday is your best friend

All paid zones in Maastricht are completely free on Sundays. If you can choose your visit day, Sunday is by far the best option. The Vrijthof square, Bonnefantenmuseum, and Maastricht's famous shops are all easily walkable from free parking.

TEFAF and busy periods

During TEFAF art fair (March), Maastricht becomes extremely busy. All P+R locations fill quickly. Book pre-paid parking months in advance or stay at a hotel with parking.

Belgian options

The Belgian town of Lanaken is 5km away with free parking. While not common, some savvy visitors park there and use the bus or taxi. More useful for Belgians visiting Maastricht than the reverse.
