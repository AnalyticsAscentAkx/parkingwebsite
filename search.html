Methodology

How this parking search works

Every result comes from the Dutch national parking register (NPR), published as open data by RDW — the same dataset parking apps and municipalities use. For each garage we compute the drive-in cost of your exact stay from the official stepped tariff tables: a 2-hour visit is priced differently from a 26-hour one, and this tool does that arithmetic for all 200+ locations at once.

Three things to know when reading the numbers. First, prices are drive-in rates — some garages offer cheaper pre-booked online rates, and some apply day caps that the register doesn't encode. Second, distances are straight-line; in a Dutch city grid, actual walking distance runs roughly 20% longer. Third, P+R sites (shown in cyan) often require a public-transport check-in to qualify for the €1–5 day rate — read our P+R guide before choosing one.
